ABOUT THE CONFERENCE


SCICON' 19, the Second International conference on Advanced Materials organized by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore will be a great platform for scientists, academicians, students and professionals from companies across the globe to share, globalize and exchange their innovative ideas in the field of advanced materials that find applications in every field of human activity focusing on a better living. The conference aims at consolidating the research findings in the specialized areas and facilitates transfer of these findings into possible prototypes and products which will enhance the quality of life for the betterment of our society.The gathering of world renowned scientist at the conference will provide a great opportunity for the young researchers to gain contacts who can indulge in productive interactions during the technical sessions. The conference will feature plenary lectures from leading experts in the field, keynote speeches, oral and poster presentations. Original research work based on the thrust areas of materials science are invited from Faculty members, Scientists, Research Scholars and Industrialists for oral or poster presentation.

Contributors are requested to submit the abstract not exceeding 300 words typed on A4 size with double line spacing and 12pt font size in Times New Roman. Abstracts of all scientific contributions will be brought out as Proceedings with ISBN number and selected contributions will be published in Scopus Indexed Journal.
